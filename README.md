# ClassMyte 📲✨  
### Send Bulk SMS using your SIM — No API, No Extra Cost

ClassMyte is a powerful app that lets you send **hundreds or even thousands of SMS using your own SIM package** — just like normal texting, but automated.

✔️ No expensive SMS services  
✔️ No per-message charges  
✔️ Works directly with your mobile network  
✔️ No data collection

## 🔥 Why ClassMyte?

Most bulk SMS tools rely on paid services like Twilio, which can be expensive and not ideal for local messaging.

ClassMyte works differently:

✅ Uses your **existing SMS bundles/packages**  
✅ Sends messages directly from your phone  
✅ Helps reduce SIM blocking using smart delay

## 🚀 Features

### 📡 Bulk SMS (Using Your SIM)
- Send SMS to **100s or 1000s of contacts**
- Uses SMS bundle instead of balance
- Works with all networks (Jazz, Zong, Telenor, etc.)

---

### ⏱️ Smart Delay System
- 5s / 10s / 30s delay options  
- Helps avoid SIM blocking  
- Safer bulk sending  

---

### ✍️ Personalized Messages
- Use variables like `[name]`  
- Each contact gets a custom message  

**Example:**  
Hello [name], your class is scheduled for today.

---

### 👥 Contact Management
- Create groups (students, customers, etc.)  
- Import contacts via Excel (`.xls`, `.xlsx`)  
- Filter active/inactive users  

---

### 📲 Background Sending
- Messages continue sending even if you leave the app  

---

## 💡 Who Is This For?

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- Uses your **SIM card to send SMS**  
- Avoid sending spam  
- Sending too fast may get SIM blocked  
- Always use delay for safer sending

2. Write your message

4. Start sending
