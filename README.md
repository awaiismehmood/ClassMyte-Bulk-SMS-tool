ClassMyte 📲✨
Send Bulk SMS using your SIM — No API, No Extra Cost

ClassMyte is a simple and powerful app that lets you send hundreds or even thousands of SMS using your own SIM package — just like normal texting, but automated.

👉 No expensive SMS services
👉 No per-message charges
👉 Works directly with your mobile network

🔥 Why ClassMyte?

Most bulk SMS tools rely on paid services like Twilio, which can be expensive and not ideal for local messaging in Pakistan.

ClassMyte does it differently:

✅ Uses your existing SMS bundles/packages
✅ Sends messages directly from your phone
✅ Helps reduce SIM blocking with smart delay controls
